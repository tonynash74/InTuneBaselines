# Android baselines (scaffold)

Folders:

- `baselines/L1/android` – L1 BYOD + Corporate baselines (example JSON populated).
- `baselines/L2/android` – L2 hardened variants (derived from L1 with stricter settings).

Types:

- `Android-*-Compliance-Policy.json` – `androidWorkProfileCompliancePolicy` or `androidDeviceOwnerCompliancePolicy`.
- `Android-*-Configuration-Profile.json` – `androidWorkProfileGeneralDeviceConfiguration` or `androidDeviceOwnerGeneralDeviceConfiguration`.

Review and adjust settings before importing into production.
